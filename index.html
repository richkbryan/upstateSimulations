How can we help you?

Upstate Simulations offers a variety of services and support to help engineers, designers, and businesses. We ensure the design of your new product is manufacturable while providing a solution to avoid issues that may occur within series production. We pride ourselves in our ability to determine if the problem is mold design based or whether it warrants a process correction. We provide our customers with computer supported solutions to help solve any problems that may arise in the design, thereby preventing these issues from spilling over into the production environment.
We are not just software people; we are die, mold, and machine designers. We will be with you 100% of the way supporting you via any means necessary. Upstate Simulations seeks to be your one stop shop for all of your injection molding needs. How do we back up such an ambitious statement you may ask? It's as easy as 1, 2, 3.
